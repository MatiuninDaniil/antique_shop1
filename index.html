Our Story

A Family Passion,
A Living Museum

Nestled in Larnaca with a breathtaking view, Faust's Attic is more than a shop — it is an experience. Run by a warm and passionate family, every corner of our space feels like a carefully curated chapter of history.

From the moment you step inside, you are surrounded by extraordinary pieces gathered from across countries and cultures — antique furniture, fine jewellery, rare artwork, vintage clothing, and countless collectibles. Browsing our collection takes hours, and that is the whole point.

"It is one of the best and biggest antique shops in Cyprus. A time travel experience with timeless treasures."

We are deeply proud of our professional restoration services — whatever you can imagine, we can bring back to its former glory. Our knowledgeable owners are always on hand with expert advice and a genuine love for their craft.
