Our Story

Dark Curated Vintage.
Quiet Luxury.

Faust's Attic reframes vintage in Cyprus with an elevated, almost editorial sense of taste — moody European chic arranged with instinctive precision.

Each piece reads like a styled moment: intentional, textural, quietly dramatic. From antique furniture and fine jewellery to rare artwork and vintage clothing, every corner feels like a carefully curated chapter of history.

"This is curation as fashion language — a space where taste becomes the narrative and every object feels ready for its own page."

Our professional restoration services are second to none — whatever you can imagine, we can bring back to its former glory. The owners are always on hand with expert advice and a genuine love for their craft.
